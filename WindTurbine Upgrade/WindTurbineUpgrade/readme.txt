WindTurbine Upgrade
by DarthPresidente

Requires:  Modern Times DLC

Use:

Adds an upgrade to Wind Turbines called: Auto Yaw Drive
Increases the electric output of the wind turbine
Cost: $1000

Installation:

Unzip the files (except this readme.txt) into your Tropico 4 folder.  Multiple copies of the mod are
included in different folders to accommodate users with different combinations
of DLC content


Your folder structure should look like...

Tropico 4\EastPoint\game\WindTurbineUpgrade.lua
Tropico 4\ex\game\WindTurbineUpgrade.lua
Tropico 4\NuclearTakedown\game\WindTurbineUpgrade.lua
Tropico 4\Propaganda\game\WindTurbineUpgrade.lua
Tropico 4\UI\BuildingUpgrates\YawDrive.tga
Tropico 4\Voodoo\game\WindTurbineUpgrade.lua
