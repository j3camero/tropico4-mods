Title: No Bells & No Fire Alarms
Source: https://www.tapatalk.com/groups/tropicomoddng/no-bells-no-fire-alarms-no-longer-supported-t365.html
Author: DarthPresidente

What it does:
Silences the excessively loud and annoying church bell sound when selecting a Church or Cathedral
Silences the fire alarm when you click on a Fire Station
WARNING: Using a mod voids eligibility for customer support from the game publisher.

To Use: place in your "Tropico 4\Game" folder. You may need to create the "Game" folder.
